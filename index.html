SalliTrack Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

SalliTrack is designed with privacy-first principles. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: September 16, 2025

1. Information We Collect

SalliTrack collects and processes the following information:

  • SMS messages from recognized bank senders only
  • Transaction details (amount, merchant, date, category)
  • App usage data for improving functionality
  • Device information necessary for app operation

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact lists or other SMS messages
  • Location data
  • Biometric information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Automatically categorize your financial transactions
  • Generate spending insights and analytics
  • Provide transaction history and summaries
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity

We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your information is protected by device security measures
  • You can delete all data at any time through app settings

4. SMS Message Processing

SalliTrack processes SMS messages as follows:

  • Only reads SMS from recognized bank senders
  • Extracts transaction information using pattern matching
  • Does not store original SMS content
  • Processes messages locally on your device
  • Automatically filters out non-transaction messages

5. Your Rights and Controls

  • View all data we have collected
  • Export your data in CSV format
  • Delete all data at any time
  • Revoke SMS permissions
  • Stop using the app and remove it
  • Request data correction or updates

6. Data Retention

  • Transaction data is retained until you delete it
  • No automatic data deletion occurs
  • You can manually clear data through settings
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered
  • App uninstallation removes all local data

7. Third-Party Services

SalliTrack does not use third-party services that access your data. The app operates entirely offline and processes all information locally on your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

SalliTrack is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Sending a notification if significant changes occur
